# How to update the website — a guide for the school office

This guide is for office staff. **No technical knowledge needed.** You can post
news, add events, manage photo albums and videos, read admission enquiries, and
update the school's contact details — all from the website's admin area.

> You'll need: a staff email that has been added to the approved list, and the
> ability to open your email. If you can't sign in, contact whoever set up the site.

---

## Signing in

1. Go to **https://srigujaratividhyalaya.com/admin**
2. Enter your **staff email** and click **"Send sign-in link"**.
3. Open your email inbox and click the **sign-in link** we just sent.
   (Open it on the **same device/browser** you requested it from.)
4. You're now in the admin dashboard. The link is one-time and expires — request
   a new one anytime. There's no password to remember.

To sign out, use **Sign out** at the bottom of the menu.

---

## Posting a news article

1. In the left menu, click **News** → **New article**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Title** (required) — the headline.
   - **Slug** — leave blank; it fills in automatically from the title.
   - **Excerpt** — a one-line summary shown in lists (optional but recommended).
   - **Body** (required) — the full article. Plain text works; basic formatting
     (paragraphs, lists, links) is supported.
   - **Cover image URL** — optional; paste an image link if you have one.
   - **Published** — tick this to make it live. Leave unticked to save a **draft**.
3. Click **Save news**. Published articles appear on the **News & Events** page
   and the homepage automatically.

To change or remove an article later: **News** → click **Edit** → make changes and
**Save**, or use **Delete article**.

---

## Adding an event

1. **Events** → **New event**.
2. Fill in **Title**, **Description**, **Starts at** (date & time), and optionally
   **Ends at** and **Location**.
3. Tick **Published** and click **Save event**.
4. Upcoming events show on the homepage and the News & Events page; past events
   drop off the "upcoming" lists automatically.

---

## Managing photo albums

1. **Gallery** → **New album**. Give it a **Title** and click **Create album**.
2. On the album page, use **Add an image**:
   - **Cloudinary id** — the identifier of the uploaded photo (your media uploader
     provides this; e.g. `sgv/sports-day-1`).
   - **Caption** and **Sort order** (lower numbers appear first) are optional.
3. Remove a photo with **Remove**, or delete the whole album at the bottom.

> Photos are hosted on **Cloudinary**. If you're unsure how to upload a photo and
> get its id, ask whoever manages the media account.

---

## Adding a video story

1. **Stories** → **New story**.
2. Enter a **Title**, a short **Caption**, and paste a **YouTube URL**
   (e.g. `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=...`). A thumbnail and play button
   appear automatically — visitors only load the video when they click it.
3. **Sort order** controls the order; tick **Published**; **Save story**.

---

## Reading admission enquiries

1. **Enquiries** shows every message from the admissions and contact forms,
   newest first, with the parent's phone and email.
2. Use the status buttons to track your follow-up:
   - **New** → just arrived (also shown as a badge on the dashboard)
   - **Contacted** → you've reached out
   - **Closed** → handled
3. New enquiries are also emailed to the office inbox automatically.

---

## Updating contact details, hero video, admissions status

1. **Settings** lets you change site-wide details:
   - **Phone, Email, Address** — these update everywhere on the site at once.
   - **Admissions open** — tick/untick to change the admissions messaging.
   - **Hero video URL** — the homepage background video (leave blank for the
     elegant gradient fallback).
   - **Prospectus PDF URL** — shown as a download on the Admissions page.
   - **Social links** — Facebook / Instagram / YouTube.
2. Click **Save settings**. Changes appear on the live site within moments.

---

## Tips

- Changes go live **automatically** — there's nothing to "publish" beyond ticking
  the **Published** box and saving.
- Save **drafts** (Published unticked) to prepare content ahead of time.
- Write clearly and check spelling — this is the school's public face.
- If something looks wrong on the live site after an edit, wait a minute and
  refresh; if it persists, contact your site administrator.
